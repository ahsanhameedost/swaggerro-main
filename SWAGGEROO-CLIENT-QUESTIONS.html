Swaggeroo — Questions for the Client

Prepared: June 15, 2026  ·  Purpose: Questions to ask Saad / Scott to lock scope and unblock the remaining work.

Target: July 1, 2026 MVP — demoable by end of June

This is a questions-only list for the meeting. See the Scope & TODO and Verified-Status documents for the detailed findings behind each point.

1. Checkout & Payments (highest priority — currently blocked)

Product add now works on local (S3 removed, switched to local storage). The one thing still failing is the checkout page — the order does not go through. Before we fix it properly we need these answers:
  1. Which payment gateway should we finalize for the MVP? Square (already half-integrated, sandbox only), or switch to Stripe / something else?
  2. Whose merchant account do we use? We need real (live) credentials — account owner, plan, and whether it's a real account or a trial/dummy.
  3. Should checkout go live (real charges) for the MVP demo, or stay in sandbox / test mode until launch?
  4. For bulk / wholesale orders — should checkout collect payment at all, or just submit a quote request (no payment, 24–48h follow-up)?
  5. What exactly should happen after a successful checkout — order confirmation email? Order appears in the admin dashboard? Anything else?
  6. Do you want guest checkout, or must the customer have an account to place an order?

2. Storage & Hosting

  1. We removed the S3 dependency and moved uploads to local storage to unblock product creation. For production, do you want us to (a) keep local, (b) re-enable S3 with your AWS account, or (c) use a different provider?
  2. Where should the app be hosted/deployed? (Vercel / Railway / your own AWS, etc.) It only runs locally now — we need a target before we can stand up a public preview.
  3. Do you own the AWS account referenced in the existing CI/CD pipeline, or should we set up fresh infrastructure?

3. Shopify vs. the current custom build

  1. The notes say the e-commerce side should use Shopify. Do you want us to migrate the store to Shopify, or keep and finish the existing custom build we already have working? (Major direction decision — it changes most of the remaining plan.)
  2. If Shopify: is that for the whole store, or only the B2C retail piece (keep custom for B2B white-label + swag builder)?

4. Swag / Package Builder (needs rebuild — exact flow unknown)

  1. Walk us through the exact intended flow of the swag/package builder, step by step.
  2. Should a customer upload their logo and see it previewed on each product (shirt, mug, bag)?
  3. Should they select multiple products in one package (current build allows only one at a time)? With per-product quantities?
  4. How should package pricing be calculated — what's the formula / rules? (The current "Calculate" button does nothing.)

5. B2B — White-label branded stores

  1. For the white-label stores, what's the exact setup — does each client get their own branded page/subdomain? How is it created (self-serve, or we set it up)?
  2. The design library — roughly how many designs at launch, and who supplies them? How does a client customize a design (logo, phrases, colours)?
  3. Revenue-share — confirm the model and split (e.g. $50 shirt → Swaggeroo $30 / client $20). How is the client's share tracked and paid out?
  4. How does a client embed/link their store from their own website?

6. B2C — Swaggeroo's own store

  1. Confirm the rule: retail = 5 units or fewer at a fixed published price; anything larger = custom hidden quote. Still correct?
  2. For the custom quote flow — what info do we collect, and what's the 24–48h turnaround process on your side?
  3. Recurring large accounts (e.g. "Key" ~5,000 shirts/year, annual calendar → events → mockups): in scope for July 1, or a later phase?

7. Fulfillment & Shipping

  1. SOA Marketing Agency (Abraham) for initial apparel fulfillment — how do orders reach them? Manual hand-off, email, or a real integration for the MVP?
  2. Do you want real carrier shipping rates (live rates via Shippo/carrier API), or are manual flat/zone rates fine for now?
  3. Are there products that ship from different places (drop-ship vs. warehouse) we need to account for?

8. Branding, Content & Pages

  1. Can you send the new kangaroo logo and brand guidelines (colours, fonts) to apply across the site?
  2. The previous build copied content/design from swagup.com. Any pages/sections flagged as legal/IP risk that must be removed or rewritten first?
  3. The 4 "Coming Soon" pages (Company, Platform, Pricing, Resources) — in scope for July 1? Who provides the copy?
  4. Testimonials and the homepage video are placeholder/dummy. Do you have real ones, or keep placeholders for the demo?

9. Scope & Priorities for July 1

  1. Of everything above, what are your top 3 must-haves for the July 1 demo vs. what can wait for a later phase?
  2. Is bulk product import (CSV/Excel upload) needed for the MVP? (The previous report claimed it was done, but it does not exist in the code — it would need to be built.)
  3. Do you need SKU codes on products? (Not currently in the system at all.)
  4. Who is the single point of contact for sign-off on scope and for answering questions during the build?
